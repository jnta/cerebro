# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Lifelong learners, researchers, and knowledge workers who value "Friction as a Feature" for deep learning and active recall.

## Product Purpose
Synapse is an AI-native cognitive gym designed for active learning. It turns isolated notes into a cohesive knowledge graph by mandating manual effort for synthesis and reflection.

## Brand Personality
"The Digital Curator": Editorial, sophisticated, high-precision, monochromatic, and architectural. It avoids the "template" look in favor of intentional asymmetry.

## Anti-references
Standard SaaS dashboards, cluttered sidebars, generic "cream-colored" UI, and any interface that prioritizes passive automation over cognitive friction.

## Design Principles
1. **Friction as a Feature**: Cognitive effort is required to commit knowledge to the vault.
2. **Editorial Precision**: Layouts should feel like a custom-designed publication, prioritizing typography and negative space.
3. **Architectural Depth**: Depth is achieved through tonal layering and stacked surfaces, never through 1px solid borders.
4. **Zen Focus**: The interface recedes during primary cognitive tasks to minimize distraction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA contrast standards, high-contrast monochromatic themes, and support for reduced motion to maintain the "Zen" atmosphere.
